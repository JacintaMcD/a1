About slammers

Slammers brings the great family sport of Beach Volleyball to Cairsville. We offer a fun, friendly atmosphere to play in along with a licenced bar for the adults and a great hire space for any function. If you are interested in joining the competition and have a team please sign up online now or contact us if you are an individual wishing to find a team or would like more information about what the services we offer.
